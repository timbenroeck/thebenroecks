Get out of my way Friday, it’s weekend time!

Posted on July 19, 2013 by Lisa

I don’t know about you, but I’m doing a happy dance because it’s Friday!!! I’m ready to get my bikini out and my shades on and to get further into my book-of-the-moment. Heck to the yes. Plus this weekend I have a tequila tasting party to go to. Yep, it’s hard to be me. Anyway, you’ll have to check back in on Monday to see the weekend recap, but it’s time for my Friday quickies.

4 Favorite Things:

  • Air conditioning
  • My bicycle (or Stella, as I affectionately call her)
  • Eating salads on patio in lovely weather
  • Riding the Husband’s motorcycle around the city at night

4 Confessions:

  • The Husband calls me ‘monkey’ quite often. He finds it endearing but I’m pretty sure it’s quite the opposite of a sexy nickname. Although at one point he called me ‘dingleberry’ which I thought was cute until I searched Google for it. I don’t recommend doing that.
  • I ate Oreos for breakfast more than once this week. I felt like it was kind of like cereal since I was dunking them in milk.
  • Going to the gym when it’s hot out makes me feel like I’m getting in shape way faster – yay for extra sweat!
  • I am incapable of writing blog posts early. As in, anytime before midnight on the day I want to post them. Bloggers who write all of their posts on weekends for the entire week, how do you do this?!

4 Favorite Links Articles:

  • On ‘socially acceptable’ sin. (via Relevant Magazine)
  • On females running marathons (via Upworthy)
  • On poverty (via The Atlantic)
  • On long distance running (via The Oatmeal)

4 Posts I Wrote This Week:

  • ABCs of Summer. (Tues)
  • Wedding recap… posing is awkward. (Wed)
  • A little conversation between Ozzie and reality. (Thurs)

Featured Blog:

This blogger shouldn’t need an introduction, but I’ll introduce her anyway!

Kalyn from Geez, Louise (formerly ‘Life as I Pretend to Know it’) is a sweet and hilarious blogger that you should have been following like, yesterday.

Reasons I love and follow Kalyn’s blog:

// She blogs about things I can totally relate to. Like, what the heck is ‘business casual‘, anyways?

// She put a picture in one post with ‘boob sweat‘ as the caption. What’s not to love about that?! I’m just not that brave.

// She enjoys reading and posts book reviews. 

// She has an inspiring list of 101 goals to complete in 1001 days. I’m exhausted just looking through it.

// She wrote this post about living with her ‘in-laws’ and I’m just in awe. I could never, in a bijillion years live with my in-laws, and so I’m intrigued by every little detail of this situation! And I for real cracked up reading about how her ‘MIL’ does her laundry.

 So if you aren’t already reading this blog (and I really have no idea why you wouldn’t be) you should get your butt (or eyes, I guess in this case) over to Geeze, Louise and check it out!

Cheers to the freakin’ weekend!
