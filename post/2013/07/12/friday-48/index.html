The week came, it was gorgeous, it was filled with friends and beers and wines and boating and an occasional summer storm and a ton of great food… and now it’s gone. 

But I can’t even be mad, because now it’s the weekend (well, after today) and that means even MORE time to enjoy absolutely everything in life.

Have I mentioned how much I love summer in Chicago?!

Aren’t these beautiful?! If you’re wondering why one is a little emptier, it’s because I clearly started drinking before I thought to take a picture. Oops!
