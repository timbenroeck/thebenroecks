Wedding recap… we’re finally at the altar!

Posted on July 3, 2013 by Lisa

Where were we on this super extended wedding recap?!

I swear, if I were a more organized blogger with scheduled posts, this recap would have only taken about a month to get through. (Someone, buy me a planner/organizer!)

However, because I have a bit of blogger ADD, you get the very strung out version.

Thanks for sticking with me.

Anyway, the last post I wrote about our wedding ended with the Husband and I at the altar.

And this was my favorite part of the wedding to plan… the ceremony!

It started off with our brother-in-law reading one of my favorite Bible verses, Colossians 3:12-17.

Then, our best friends read this lovely reading, which they selected and I thought was absolutely perfect:

And finally, it was time for the vows! The Husband (or Tim, as these vows refer to him as) gets credit for writing these.

And just like that, with a few more words from the pastor and the exchanging of some (gorgeous) rings, we were husband and wife!

And so began forever.

Yay!

Honestly, the ceremony went by in a complete blur. I felt like it lasted about 4 seconds. In actuality, it probably lasted closer to 15 minutes. It definitely wasn’t drawn out, but I thought it was very sentimental and sweet.

Is that enough pictures for a Wednesday?! 

I think so!
