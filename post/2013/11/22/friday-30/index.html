I think this is my last week of denial that winter is here.

Posted on November 22, 2013 by Lisa

I know I’ve said this before, but I freakin’ love Thanksgiving. It’s my favorite holiday of the year and now it’s only six days away – yippeee! Let the search for stretchy pants begin! (I’m totally not joking about this, I consider eating to be serious business).

I was especially thankful for this last week. I had some great conversations with my church group ladies; the Husband and I spent some quality time with our friends who are expecting their first child in April (MORE BABIES!); I had the chance to spend some time with my mom (which doesn’t usually happen during the week); and the Husband and I celebrated Friendsgiving with four other couples. It was a super-successful Friendsgiving because everyone brought delicious foods and all the Thankgiving basics were covered and I ate a lot and it was magical.

But, now it’s time for the weekend and I am thrilled because the Husband and I have plans to see some of our favorite people and I could just not be happier about catching up with them!

So, onto Friday Fours!

 The Husband and I got these ‘romantic’ fortunes on our anniversary. Nothing says ‘awwww’ like Panda Express telling you to see other people.

4 Confessions:

  • I just discovered ‘Divorce Court’, and let me tell you, it has become my new go-to show when I’m feeling stuck in my own life. These people are CRAZY and watching the show makes me instantly feel better about whatever situation I’m in.
  • I’m always embarrassed when I need to leave a urine sample and I’m dehydrated so my pee is basically gold. BUT, I was at my OB/GYN checkup and saw a bunch of samples in the lab and they were ALL bright yellow / gold, so I’m clearly not the only one not drinking enough water. Phew!
  • I am that person who will have a symptom, check the internet for said symptom, and be fully convinced she’s dying, even if the disease only affects .1% of the population.
  • Cranberry sauce is one of my favorite foods at Thanksgiving. I even love the kind straight from the can. Yummmmm!
