Well that was fast. #thatswhatshesaid

Posted on June 28, 2013 by Lisa

Yes, I just used a hashtag in the title of a blog post. I’m pretty sure that’s breaking quite a few social media rules.

Sorry ’bout that.

This post is really not starting off on the right foot, except I think I get a pass because it’s Friday. And that’s what the title of this post refers to… that I just can’t believe how fast this week came and went! With all of the hockey viewing, socializing, and political drama that I watched go down, my week flew by.

Life is good.

Shall we get on with the Friday Four routine, now?!

Yes, I think we shall.

4 Favorite Things:

  • Chats with best friends.
  • Red and white gummy bears. 
  • Summer storms. 
  • The Blackhawks… because they freakin’ won the Stanley Cup!

4 Confessions:

  • I’m having a country music type of week. As in I want to listen to it non-stop. Don’t judge me too harshly for this, please.
  • I swear, my brain is on summer vacation. I find myself saying the most awkward sentences lately. It’s like my mind has totally forgotten how to form complete thoughts, and also how to spell.
  • I stayed up way too late (multiple nights this week) to watch sports and politics. The sleep deprivation caused me to miss quite a few early morning gym sessions. It was most definitely worth it, but I am one tired and sluggish-feeling little lady.
  • I’m still freaked out by how quickly years of my life are passing. I really cannot believe how long ago I was in college. Ummmm hey 27 year old self, you really aren’t 22 anymore! Yikes! When did this growing up thing happen?!
