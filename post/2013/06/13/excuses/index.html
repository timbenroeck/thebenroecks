Excuses, excuses (I have plenty of them!)

Posted on June 13, 2013 by Lisa

Lately I’ve been really good (if I do say so myself!) at writing my posts ahead of time. Like I’ve been writing a post a whole day before I plan to publish it!

But sometimes I fail.

And last night was one of those nights. So let me just explain myself:

OK, maybe I don’t even need a post. This picture pretty much says it all.

Reasons I couldn’t write a super-interesting post today.

*Except this list can really be used on any given day*

  • I was too busy reading (and sometimes even commenting on) your blog posts to write my own.
  • I was drinking Malbec and it was delicious so I had more than one glass and then writing a blog post became a very distant concept and life priority.
  • I was taking selfies. (See above picture).
  • I was working on sponsor/swap options (coming soon! Eeeek I’m nervous about this one.).
  • I was finding other blogs to sponsor.
  • I was drinking out of my new Vino2Go cup so that I can write a review sometime this year.
  • I was watching the Blackhawks play the Bruins in the Stanley Cup Finals.
  • I was bothering the Husband.
  • I was making dessert.
  • I was eating dessert (it was delicious).
  • I was taking a picture of my family.

I really need more photogenic subjects. And also to start using the fancy-pants camera I bought instead of my not-as-fancy iPhone ‘camera’.

  • It was raining. No, it wasn’t raining in my apartment, it was raining outside. Somehow I still think this could be an acceptable excuse.
  • I was tweeting.
  • I was emailing.
  • I was Facebook-ing.
  • I was trash-talking (about hockey) via text message with my Bostonian cousin.
  • The little Monster was barking and I couldn’t concentrate.
  • I was working on a post for Friday even though it was Wednesday and I didn’t even have a post for Thursday yet (yes, I just named three days of the week in this sentence. Please try to stay with me here.)

… And those were just my excuses for last night! Given this very impressive list, it’s really amazing I actually have a blog at all.

Cheers for wonderful weekday nights!
