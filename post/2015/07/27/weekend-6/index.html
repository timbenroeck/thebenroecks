A weekend zooming around town.

Posted on July 27, 2015 by Lisa

My weekend looked a little something like this:

… sunshine.

… small group with our church community.

… an ice cream date.

… reading.

… early, early mornings that were not-by-choice.

… bike riding around the city, with Clara in tow.

(We bought this helmet for Clara and this baby seat that we absolutely love.)

… church.

… playing at the playground every day, multiple times a day.

… a public pool.

… amazing finds at a second hand store.

… ‘book club’ aka ‘a time to eat and chat with friends and occasionally mention a book’.

… the farmers market in which I was so ravenous I ate an entire pizza by myself (but Clara ate the crusts).

… the movie ‘Gone Girl’, which impressed me with how closely it followed the book.

Clearly, our weekend was packed with summer goodness and I wouldn’t have it any other way. Well, I guess I would make it longer, but we can’t have everything! I hope your weekend was filled to the brim with all good things, too!
