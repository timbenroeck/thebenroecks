Maybe I’m ready to get back into our ‘routine’. Maybe not.

Posted on January 3, 2015 by Lisa

Tomorrow is the last day of our very-extended holiday break, and I can’t decide if I’m ready to get back to ‘real life’ or not. I love the holiday season, of course. However, we don’t travel for it so we end up spending a lot of time at home – in a staycation of sorts. It was fun. We went ice skating, we saw friends, we saw family, we relaxed, we spent time with Clara, we did some things around the house, we cooked and ate food, we went out for brunch, we drank champagne.. but all good things must come to an end, and I’m a little excited to get back to a normal routine.

I think this is partly because I’m unsure of what our routine even looks like at this point. I mean, how will me and Clara pass the time without Tim with us full-time? I don’t know! I’m anxious to get involved in things with some sort of consistency, I guess that’s really what I’m excited about.

But if we won the lottery and Tim could retire early, I wouldn’t be sad about it, no sir.
