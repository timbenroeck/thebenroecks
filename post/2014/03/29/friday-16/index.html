Avoiding the maternity section and other thoughts from this week.

Posted on March 29, 2014 by Lisa

Compared to last week and last weekend, this week was a breeze! Sure, work was a bit stressful, but the Husband and I had two nights ‘off’ in which we went Target shopping and watched March Madness and caught up on life stuff. I also made it to barre class twice this week and love it more and more each time, except I’m truly awful at it. Oh well, I can only improve, right?! I had two fun social nights with my lady friends, which made the week extra-special. I love my girl time.

The past few days, it’s been rainy and awful in Chicago (same old story that I’ve been telling for the past few months, except now it’s rain instead of snow) and I think it’s actually helped to relax me.

I know it’s helped Oz relax! (Not that he needed much assistance.)

 

Four thoughts from this week

  • I spent a few minutes one morning deleting a large number of my Facebook friends that I don’t feel like I really ‘know’ anymore. Basically, I decided if the person is not someone I’d go up to if I saw them walking down the street, they got deleted. Well, then I realized how ironic it is that I’m ‘friends’ with hundreds of people I don’t actually ‘know’ on Twitter/Instagram/Bloglovin’, etc and that I have my life on public display on the internet, but I don’t want people who at-one-point-actually-knew-me to see my Facebook pictures. I can’t explain it, people, it’s just how I am!
  • I made the small change of doing squats while brushing my teeth. Sometimes I do 25, sometimes I do 50 in the 2 minutes it takes my electric toothbrush to tell me I’m done. It’s a minor thing, but it makes me feel much better to know I get some exercise in every day.
  • Brackets are bustin’ everywhere! I’m still loving this March Madness. (I still have all of my Final Four teams in the tournament! Crossing my fingers.)
  • In our last apartment, no one could see into our bedroom from the outside. In this apartment, our bedroom windows face the apartment next door. I’m still not adjusted to this and the lovely people who live on the floor below us (who we share a backyard with) and the family next door have seen me looking far more disheveled / disrobed than I would like them to see me.

 

Four pregnancy notes (next week)

  • Remember how I said the pregnancy bump is out? Well, no one else (except close friends and the Husband, obviously) can see it, evidently, which is getting annoying to me because strangers (and some friends) keep saying “Really?! You’re 6 months already?! You don’t look (that) pregnant!” I’m sure this is a compliment or something, but at this point I’m almost into my third trimester (7th month) and I’m feeling very, very pregnant, so I just don’t want anyone commenting on how I look unless it’s along the lines of “Wow, you look beautiful!” and then I will happily take the compliment.
  • Oh pregnancy hormones… I’m not sure if I just had a rough week, but I was emotional last week for the first time all pregnancy, and it was ugly.
  • I’m avoiding the maternity section at all costs. However, this pregnancy has been a great excuse for me to buy cute dresses (especially maxi dresses) that I plan on living in once I outgrow my pants. Yes, I may be wearing the same outfits over and over and over again, but only for a month or two! At least that’s the plan at this point. This is, of course, subject to change when I actually grow out of my ‘normal’ clothes.
  • The Husband and I watched ‘The Business of Being Born‘ and I cannot recommend it enough. Sure, the conversation is largely one-sided, but it’s jam-packed with information that I think every pregnant or thinking-of-becoming pregnant mom would want to know.

Featured blog(ger)

I absolutely love Ashten’s blog (Always Ashten) and if you aren’t already reading every single brilliant post she writes, then I’m excited to tell you that you’ve been missing out and now have the opportunity to catch up.
