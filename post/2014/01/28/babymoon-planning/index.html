Our next adventure abroad is booked!

Posted on January 28, 2014 by Lisa

If it’s dinner time and I still haven’t blogged for the day, is it best to write a blog post or save it for tomorrow?

Age old question, people.

I’ll just go ahead and write/publish it tonight, because darnit, I have things to say (per usual)!

Well, work has been busy these past few days, and I had to work all day on Saturday. Working on a Saturday does not thrill me, even if it involves getting paid overtime. BUT, there was something else I did on Saturday, while sitting at my computer in my family room, glaring at Microsoft Excel… (not that I’m holding a grudge against that entire program, or anything).

I booked our next big vacation!

As soon as I found out we were going to be parents this year, the first thing I thought of (well, the first rational thought I had) was: Where do I need to travel in the next nine months and how am I going to fit it all in?!

You see, it’s no secret that I love to travel.

Love, love, love it. 

And the Husband and I usually do several bigger trips a year. In 2011 it was Boston and New York City,Colorado (for skiing), Washington DC, Los Angeles and Jamaica.

In 2012 it was Aspen (skiing), San Francisco/Napa/Yosemite/Los Angeles, Jamaica (wedding), Tanzania (honeymoon), and Istanbul (also honeymoon).

In 2013 it was Hawaii, Boston, and Argentina/Uruguay.

In 2014 it’s going to be Charleston and our newly booked trip…

Central Europe!

We were originally planning on going to Thailand this year, but with being newly pregnant when I had to book the trip, I decided I’d rather book a trip that I just felt a little more comfortable with. And I have always wanted to visit Central Europe!

Which is what a vacation is all about, right?!
