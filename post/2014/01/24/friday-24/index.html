My dog totally judges my selfie taking skills (or lack thereof).

Posted on January 24, 2014 by Lisa

This week has tired me out, folks, both emotionally and physically and I guess mentally, too. Yep, let’s just say I’m worn out. I’m doing that thing I tend to do where I take on way too many ‘obligations’ and I can barely keep track of them all, much less follow through on them!

This week was filled with: working (I worked the last two weekends and quite a few nights in addition to my 40 hrs per week); church groups; blogging (obviously); spending some quality time with the Husband; working out (very occasionally); sleeping when I have a few hours to spare (usually after 1am); and trying to stay on top of my social life. Phew. I guess it sounds pretty typical for a 20-something person, but I think I’m getting old because I just don’t have the energy I used to!

Ozzie was totally judging my wannabe-pregnant-belly-selfie pic. See that judging face?!

But enough complaining from me! I have to say, it was a good week overall and it flew by. I’m thankful for all the people I was able to see and the things I was able to do and the health I enjoyed and this great (and frigid) city I live in with the Husband and our little Monster!

I hope you feel blessed this week, too.

Let’s move on to Friday Fours, shall we?!

4 Favorite Things:

  • More daylight hours! (It was light until after 5pm today and I loved it. Even though I was stuck in my office.)
  • Cuties. The mini-oranges, that is.
  • Laughter.
  • Talking to other pregnant or recently pregnant ladies. Or anyone who will listen to me talk about this little fetus person, really.

 

4 Confessions:

  • Microsoft Excel has the power to make me cry. I didn’t burst into tears at work this week, but I was too close for comfort.
  • I’m jealous that Ozzie definitely loves the Husband more than me. The Husband is convinced if I walked him or fed him then I’d be the new favorite, but I’m just not willing to put in the effort.
  • It’s totally common for me not to eat anything until 3pm. I’m just not a morning eater, unless it’s a quick piece of fruit or a Jolly Rancher. I love Jolly Ranchers.
  • I’m now considering any amount of physical activity a workout. Last night I almost didn’t go to CrossFit, because I got home at 6:15 and open gym closes at 7pm. WELL, I was determined that work would not defeat my exercise schedule, so off to the gym I went. For 25 minutes. I guess something is better than nothing, right?

 

4 Pregnancy Notes:

  • The Husband and I (and let’s be honest… mainly my mom) are picking out the paint color for the baby’s room this week, because we’re having our new apartment painted before we move in. Things are getting REAL.
  • We hear the baby’s heartbeat for the first time this morning. Send positive thoughts our way, please! We would love your prayers.
  • First babymoon is booked. Second babymoon is in the works.
  • I feel like a fake pregnant woman, since I don’t have a belly and don’t really feel very pregnant. I have a feeling this shall pass soon enough!
